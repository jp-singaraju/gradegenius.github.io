Collecting and Using Your Data

This app is intended for FISD students who wish to view their class grades by mobile device. This may include elementary school, middle school, and high school students. In order to completely access the features within this app, the user must provide the login credentials to his/her FISD HAC Gradebook. The app then contacts the HAC server and processes the information in an encrypted server. The data is then aesthetically displayed on the user's phone. The information stored on the local phones files is not visible by anyone except the user. All content within the app is appropriate for use by children and does not contain any inappropriate information. All requirements satisfy the Google Play Console Family Policy for children.
